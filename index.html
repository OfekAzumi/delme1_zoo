Zootopia On Earth

Come to the Panda, Eagle, and Reindeer Zoo!

Where else can you see these three amazing animals in one place? Our zoo is home to a rare giant panda, a majestic bald eagle, and a playful reindeer.
Our panda, Mei Mei, is a gentle giant who loves to eat bamboo.
She's also very playful, and you can often see her rolling around in the snow or playing with her toys.
Our eagle, Baldy, is a powerful bird of prey. He can soar up to 10,000 feet in the air, and his vision is eight times better than ours.
Baldy is also very intelligent, and he loves to play with his food.
Our reindeer, Rudolph, is a friendly and festive animal. He loves to eat carrots and apples, and he's always up for a game of fetch.
Rudolph also has a very special gift: he can light up his nose at Christmastime!
In addition to these three amazing animals, our zoo is home to over 1,000 other animals from all over the world.
We have lions, tigers, bears, elephants, giraffes, and so much more.
We also have a variety of educational exhibits, so you can learn about the animals and their habitats. And we have a variety of fun activities, like the petting zoo, the carousel, and the train ride.
So come to the Panda, Eagle, and Reindeer Zoo! You'll have an amazing time exploring our zoo and learning about these amazing animals.
